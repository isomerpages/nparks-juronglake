<h3><strong>Nurturing Creativity for All at Jurong Lake Gardens</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Arts@JLG: explore</strong> is a 4-day event taking place from 29
May to 1 June 2025 at Jurong Lake Gardens.
<br>
<br>The event celebrates diversity and inclusion, bringing the community together
through thoughtfully curated arts and nature-themed programmes.&nbsp;
<br>
<br>It welcomes various community members, including seniors and persons with
disabilities, not just as participants but as contributors who share their
unique talents and perspectives through special performances and showcases.</p>

here:</p>
