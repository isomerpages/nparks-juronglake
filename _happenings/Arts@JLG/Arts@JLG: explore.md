<h2>Nurturing Creativity for All at Jurong Lake Gardens</h2>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Arts@JLG: explore</strong> is an inclusive community event that
highlights Jurong Lake Gardens as a welcoming space for all. This programme
offers a range of activities designed to engage diverse groups, such as
the elderly and individuals with special needs.</p>
<p>Through interactive workshops, nature walks, and creative sessions, <em>Arts@JLG: explore</em> encourages
participants to connect, express themselves, and discover the therapeutic
power of art in the natural world. By promoting inclusion and creativity,
this event aims to foster a sense of belonging and strengthen community
ties in a supportive environment.</p>
