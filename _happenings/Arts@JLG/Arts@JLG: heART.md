<h2><strong>Nurturing Wellness Through Nature's Palette</strong></h2>
<p><strong>Arts@JLG: heART</strong> is an event that invites visitors to embark
on a transformative journey of creative expression and holistic well-being
amidst the serene beauty of Jurong Lake Gardens. This event offers a variety
of interactive art activities, workshops, and mindfulness experiences designed
to nurture the body, mind, and spirit.</p>
<p>Set against the backdrop of lush greenery and tranquil landscapes, Arts@JLG:
heART encourages participants to connect with nature through artistic exploration.
Whether you're discovering new creative outlets, engaging in therapeutic
arts, or simply soaking in the peaceful surroundings, this event offers
a rejuvenating experience for all.</p>
<p>Come and be inspired to express yourself, unwind, and rediscover the power
of art and nature in promoting well-being. Check out highlights from our
2024 edition below!</p>
