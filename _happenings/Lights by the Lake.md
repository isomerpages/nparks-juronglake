<p><em>Highlights of Lights by the Lake 2024</em>
</p>
<p>A signature annual event at Jurong Lake Gardens, <strong>Lights by the Lake</strong> presents
a captivating gala of lights, music, and cultural delights in celebration
of the Mid-Autumn Festival.</p>
<p>Be awed by the larger-than-life lantern displays, cultural showcases and
music performances, and more!</p>
<h3><strong>Find out more information on 2025's edition </strong><a href="https://lightsbythelake.nparks.gov.sg/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">here</a><strong>.</strong></h3>

<p><em>Lights by the Lake is organised by National Parks Board. This edition of Lights by the Lake is held in conjunction with SG60.</em>
