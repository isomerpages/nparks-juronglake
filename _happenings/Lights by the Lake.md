<h2><strong>Celebrating Mid-Autumn at Jurong Lake Gardens</strong></h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Lights by the Lake/LBTL_2024_Lanterns.jpg">

<p>A signature annual event at Jurong Lake Gardens, <strong>Lights by the Lake</strong> presents
a captivating gala of lights, music, and cultural delights in celebration
of the Mid-Autumn Festival.</p>
<p>Be awed by the larger-than-life lantern displays, cultural showcases and
music performances, and more! Check out highlights from our 2024 edition
below.</p>
