<h4>Discover spaces for the community!</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Gardenhouse" src="/images/Lakeside Garden/Gardenhouse/Gardenhouse_cropped.jpg">
</div>
<p>Gardenhouse has 2 multi-purpose halls (Banyan and Mempat Halls) and 1
function room (Neram Room) providing community space where visitors can
gather for activities like gardening workshops, nature appreciation talks,
and crafts for children.</p>

<p>All the halls and rooms are also available for rental to organise events
