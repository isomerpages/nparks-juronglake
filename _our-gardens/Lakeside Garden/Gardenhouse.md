<h4>Discover spaces for the community!</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Gardenhouse" src="/images/Lakeside Garden/Gardenhouse/Gardenhouse_cropped.jpg">
</div>
<p>Gardenhouse has 2 multi-purpose halls (<a href="https://www.nparks.gov.sg/juronglakegardens/connect-with-us/venue-hire/banyan-hall" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Banyan Hall</a>,
<a href="https://www.nparks.gov.sg/juronglakegardens/connect-with-us/venue-hire/mempat-hall" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Mempat Hall</a>) and 1 function room (<a href="https://www.nparks.gov.sg/juronglakegardens/connect-with-us/venue-hire/neram-room" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Neram Room</a>),
providing community space where visitors can gather for activities like
gardening workshops, nature appreciation talks, and crafts for children.</p>
