<h4>A unique waterfront venue for performance and community</h4>

<p>Lakeside Green, which spans approximately 4,000m<sup>2</sup>&nbsp;is a
spacious lawn area that serves as a unique waterfront venue for musical
performances and concerts, or a gathering space for community activities
amidst greenery.</p>
