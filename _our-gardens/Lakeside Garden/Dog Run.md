<p>The Dog run is a 2,200sqm grass field where dogs are allowed to run freely.
One of the features requested for by the public during the public engagement
exercises, the Dog Run provides washing areas and trash bins for owners
to clean up after their dogs, while the grass mounds present provide natural
obstacles for dogs to enjoy. All dog lovers are welcome!</p>
